About Me

Programming is all about logic and the structure of a language. During this coder dojo HTML session we will teach HTML and CSS, while introducing you to logic in JavaScript and JQuery. You want to learn to build dynamic and cool websites well then you have come to the right place. Over the next few months you will learn how to build a slick looking site, add audio to your site, display videos and build web games. This is an exciting adventure and your imagination for the Internet will drive this course.
